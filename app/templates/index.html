Chef Stacy Simonson-Crowe


Stacy Crowe-Simonson, Executive Chef, a native of Brownsville, Texas, began her culinary career at Chez nous in 1994; in 1998, she continued her training at “Chateau des Reynats,” a prestigious Michelin-starred establishment in Perigeux, France. She then joined Fabrice Poigin at “Vignola” in San Diego before returning home to Chez nous. She brings great balance and boundless enthusiasm to the kitchen. Her strong classical training shows thru in her flawlessly executed dishes, and her most innovative offerings are simply delicious. In Stacy’s kitchen, the food tastes like itself.
